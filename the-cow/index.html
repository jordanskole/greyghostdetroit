We packed the house at Yeman's Street

Combined with the french onion goodness packed into the agnolotti pasta, mustard seeds and rich onion broth, the dish might be one of the best things I've eaten in Detroit or Hamtramck. It was absolutely the purest midwest comfort food, in my experience, and the first time a chef delivered on a stylistic promise made prior to a restaurant opening.
